What are the drums?

Drums are a type of percussion instrument that produce sound when a stretched membrane,
or "drumhead," is struck with a hand, sticks, or mallets. They are a foundational element
in music, providing rhythm and setting the tempo. A common setup is the drum kit, a
collection of various drums and cymbals that a single player can perform on.

There are many extensions you can add to the drum kit but here are the main ones

  • High tom
  • Middle tom
  • Hi-hat
  • Floor drum
